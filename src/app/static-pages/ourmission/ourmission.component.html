THE START

When Michael, our CEO, started brainstorming project ideas to harness the power of cloud computing in marine technology, it took a while to think of ideas. However, he soon realized that much of the marine industry was ripe for disruption by artificial intelligence and big data techniques. Michael is enamored with AI, and he saw BioSeer as a project to build his AI skills and use them for good.

.

GETTING THE TEAM

Michael pitched the idea to the members of the BioSeer. The team quickly got together and collaborated to build the project to where it is today. With a diverse array of backgrounds, the BioSeer team was ready to take on the challenge.

FIRST STEPS

One of the first steps the team took was planning out the product to the detail. Writing out the user experience, target market, and definable goals of the product, the BioSeer team set a clear path forward. Market research on competitors established the uniqueness of BioSeer further.

.

DEVELOPMENT

BioSeer as you see it now is the product of two weeks worth of development. The developers have been working non-stop to get BioSeer where it is today.
