Our Team

The BioSeer Development team is a subset of Rovotics, a 17 person underwater robotics company based in Sacramento California. There are five key members of the development team. Heading the project as CEO, Michael Equi organizes the vision for the overall project and is the main AI and ML developer. Jaiveer Gahunia also develops the AI. Caelin Sutch heads the Web Interface, with Alden Parker providing front end support. James Monroe is in charge of our hardware.

Rovotics is a high school academic team specializing in the design, manufacture, and operation of underwater Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). Our team is comprised of highly-skilled members with multiple years of experience, providing us with the expertise to tackle a variety of challenges. Over the past decade, Rovotics has successfully produced ROVs suited to a variety of missions, including exploration of shipwrecks in the Great Lakes in 2014 and scientific research and oilfield maintenance in the Arctic in 2015.
