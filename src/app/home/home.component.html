Revolutionary Marine Monitoring

BioSeer's mobile buoy integrates seamlessly into the system of BioSeer products. Designed for long deployment times, the BioSeer mobile buoy uses adaptive buoyancy systems and thrusters to navigate and explore its enviornment, allowing users to take a larger scope of data than ever before.
